ServiceNow (SNOW) Ticketing System for CPRS Issues

YourIT Portal has replaced the CAC LEAF System
Affects all CPRS end users
⚠️ Important: All CPRS issues, requests, and updates must be submitted through the YourIT portal. Do not submit via email or instant message.

📋 How to submit a ticket

1
Locate the YourIT icon on your desktop and open the portal.
2
Determine your ticket type:
⚡ Incident
Something is broken or producing errors — select   Report an Issue
🔄 Change Request
New, removing, or updating documents — select   Submit a Request
3
Complete the "Report an Issue" or VISTA/CPRS Request Form, then click Order Now.
